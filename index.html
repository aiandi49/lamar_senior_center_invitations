Teach AI to Your Seniors

Ten of Tucson's most respected senior centers and community organizations have been hand-selected to receive this exclusive, complimentary offer: a live, in-person AI education session — free of charge — for their members in 2026.

The Initiative

Bridging the Gap Between Seniors and Artificial Intelligence

Every week, millions of older Americans hear about AI — on the news, from their grandchildren, in passing conversation — and feel left behind. The truth is, AI is one of the most powerful time-saving tools ever created, and seniors deserve access to it just as much as anyone else.

Lamar Myers, an independent AI educator and technology consultant based in Tucson, Arizona, is personally reaching out to senior community centers across Pima County with a simple, no-cost offer: come in, set up a screen, and show your members how AI can make their daily lives easier, richer, and more connected.

No sales pitch. No products. No strings. Just knowledge — given freely, as a gift to the community.
